PURPOSE: To illustrate techniques for generating random numbers nonuniformly, generating distributions from data, some common techniques in fitting curves, quantifying the goodness of fit via KS testing, and error bars.  

CONTENTS:  
picking random numbers.nb:  Common pitfalls when choosing uniformly in disks or spheres.
uniform.nb:  How do you know if a distribution is actually uniform?  
exponential.nb:  How can you choose a random number from an exponential distribution?
scale free.nb:  How can you choose from a power law distribution?  
error bars.nb:  Determining standard error and 95%CIs.
